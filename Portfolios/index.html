🥉 3rd Prize UNODC Forensic CTF 2022

May 2022 · CTF Competition

  • Description

    UNODC Forensic CTF is a competition organized by UNODC for africa in Digital Forensics. Digital Forensics is the process of identifying, preserving, analysing and documenting digital evidence to be presented in a court of law and more than 60% of people in Africa access the internet through their mobile phones. This means that most digital forensics probes on the continent are done on mobile devices. It was only right that we organize the competition to meet the realities on the ground. With the efforts of my team and me, we won third prize.
